Privacy Policy for Simple Gambling Game

Your privacy is important to us. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

The extension is solely a local game that runs in your browser, and all game data (such as dice rolls and virtual currency) remains on your device. No information is sent to or stored on external servers.

By using this extension, you acknowledge that no personal data is collected.
